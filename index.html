Purpose of Life

We live day to day during the same struggles of birth, old age, disease and death in which everyone in the parallel universes suffers. The universe is full of different lifeforms with unique material combinations beyond our current physical abilities to understand but we may achieve awereness via consciouness and by looking at the instructions left behind. The instructions are in the Bhagavad-gita(1972-Prabhupada). This source code comes directly verifiable from God Himself. The Age of Spiritual Science is here. The Puranas in the Vedas are the original history of our plane. This is the Holy Grail. It has all the info on everything and lacks nothing. All proven by "modern" science. There is now a clear understanding of our true, original origins. The instructions tell us that our purpose is to go back home, back to Godhead.

Why should we care

To be conscious is the main sign of being a spiritual entity. No entity is divided from its source at any time. So is the purpose of life to survive it or to reach a position in understanding that results in peace with bliss and true identity? We have an eternal life and we have the choice to move into eternity, to choose otherwise would be a loss and great pain. In order to appreciate such sublime thinking is to actually be in service of the Supreme Person. Our best interest is at hand, to solve the issue of repeated birth and death. To stop being in a miserable concept of reality. The entire current situation is that we come from nothing and go to nothing. A bleak and dark, and boring, ontological view. Reality is much more complex, beautiful and full than this impersonal vodism. Plus there is no need for guesswork or theory. The compiled program is already in full effect.
